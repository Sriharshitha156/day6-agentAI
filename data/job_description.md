# Junior AI Engineer — TechVest Corp

## About TechVest
TechVest is a fast-growing fintech startup building AI-powered investment tools for retail investors. We are looking for a Junior AI Engineer to join our 8-person AI team and help build the next generation of our recommendation and fraud-detection systems.

## Responsibilities
- Design and implement ML models for investment recommendation and fraud detection
- Build and maintain data pipelines for real-time financial data processing
- Collaborate with the product team to deploy models to production
- Write clean, tested Python code with proper documentation
- Participate in code reviews and team stand-ups

## Requirements
- **Python** — strong proficiency (3+ years hands-on)
- **Machine Learning** — solid understanding of supervised/unsupervised learning, feature engineering, model evaluation
- **Frameworks** — hands-on experience with scikit-learn, pandas, numpy
- **Deep Learning** — familiarity with PyTorch or TensorFlow
- **Data Engineering** — experience with SQL, data pipelines, ETL processes
- **Communication** — ability to explain technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders
- **Education** — B.Tech/M.Tech in CS, AI, ML, or related field

## Nice-to-Have
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS/GCP/Azure)
- Knowledge of MLOps practices (DVC, MLflow, CI/CD for ML)
- Familiarity with NLP or time-series forecasting
- Open-source contributions

## Why Join TechVest
- Work on real problems that impact millions of investors
- Mentorship from senior AI engineers
- Competitive salary + equity
- Remote-first culture
